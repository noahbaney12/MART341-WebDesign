Theodore Seuss Geisel, better known as Dr. Seuss, was one of the greatest and most influential authors of all time.

He wrote countless children's classics including The Cat in the Hat, Horton Hears a Who, How the Grinch Stole Christmas, The Lorax, and my personal favorite, Green Eggs and Ham.

His numerous writings have earned him dozens of awards and he truly was one of the greatest authors of the 20th century.
