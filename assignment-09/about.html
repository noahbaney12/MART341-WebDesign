Necktie Instructable

Why did I choose this project?

I had to tie ties a lot when I was in high school and would constantly have to look up how to tie one. Eventually, I had so much practice that I didn't need a tutorial anymore. Now I am just trying to pay it forward with tutorials for those who may need it.

What does it mean to me?

It means that young men and women will have access to life skills like being able to tie a tie, even if they may not have a role model figure in their lives to teach them personally.
